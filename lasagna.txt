Certainly! Here's a delicious vegetarian lasagna recipe for you:

**Ingredients:**

For the Tomato Sauce:
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
- 1 can (14 ounces) diced tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons tomato paste
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)

For the Spinach and Ricotta Filling:
- 1 pound (450g) fresh spinach, chopped
- 2 cups ricotta cheese
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 egg
- Salt and black pepper to taste

Additional Ingredients:
- 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
- 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

**Instructions:**

1. **Prepare the Tomato Sauce:**
   - In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions and garlic, and sauté until onions are translucent.
   - Add crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, black pepper, and sugar (if using). Simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

2. **Prepare the Spinach and Ricotta Filling:**
   - In a separate pan, wilt the chopped spinach over medium heat until it's cooked down. Drain any excess liquid.
   - In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan, one egg, and the cooked spinach. Season with salt and pepper. Mix well.

3. **Assemble the Lasagna:**
   - Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
   - In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the bottom.
   - Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top.
   - Spread half of the spinach and ricotta filling over the noodles, followed by a portion of tomato sauce and a layer of mozzarella cheese.
   - Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of tomato sauce and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella cheese on top.

4. **Bake:**
   - Cover the lasagna with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes.
   - Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the edges are golden brown.

5. **Let it Rest:**
   - Allow the lasagna to rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.

6. **Serve:**
   - Slice, serve, and enjoy your delicious vegetarian lasagna!

Feel free to add other vegetables like zucchini, mushrooms, or bell peppers to the layers for extra flavor and nutrition.
